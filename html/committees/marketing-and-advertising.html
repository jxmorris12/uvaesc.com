Marketing and Advertising

The Marketing and Advertising Committee is responsible for broadcasting ESC events to the SEAS community and branding ESC events. In doing so, the committee determines the best means to reach out to different communities within SEAS. The committee is engaged in all forms of advertising including chalking, flyering, and video production, as well as developing new and effective advertising strategies.
