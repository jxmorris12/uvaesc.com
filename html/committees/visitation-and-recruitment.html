Visitation and Undergraduate Recruitment

The Visitation and Undergraduate Recruitment Committee is responsible for providing daily tours of SEAS for prospective undergraduate students. The committee coordinates these tours with the Office of Undergraduate Admission, the SEAS Office of Undergraduate Programs, and the University Guide Service. The visitation committee is also responsible for providing representation for SEAS at various recruitment functions throughout the university.

Schedule: Tours are held every Monday-Saturday at 1pm in front of Thornton Hall A-Wing. An additional 3pm Friday tour is given for students wishing to take a later tour time. Any changes to the tour schedule will be posted to the Engineering Student Council Facebook page.

Interested in Joining E-Guides? Applications and trial tours will be conducted in Fall 2016.

The 2016-17 Director of Visitation is Casey Hartless.
